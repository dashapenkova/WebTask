About me

Hi! My name is Dasha Penkova and I am a student of the Ivan Franko National University of Lviv.

Like any student, I study a lot, spend time with my friends, do sport, expand my horizons. Of course, I have a hobby. I really like taking photos of a beautiful sky, sunsets and sunrises.

I do it just for myself and enjoy it.

Read more

I love the evening sky with all my heart. I admire every sunset. I marvel at every sunset. I take pictures of every sunset.

I am a person who adores the sky. For me, it is so romantic and powerful at the same time ...

When I see a sunset, which merges with the water, I always feel somehow infinitely free ... in thoughts, in movements...

To some extent, I am happy that I do not have the opportunity to see the sun touching the horizon from the shore of a sea or a lake every day ... because in this way these moments become more special, more magical ... and are etched in my memory for a long time, if not forever.

I adore heaven, and I will not cease to say this..
